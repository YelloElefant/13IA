2023 NZSS U15 Tournament

Headmaster Welcome

As Headmaster of Hamilton Boys’ High School, I am pleased to take this opportunity to welcome you to the 2023 New Zealand Secondary Schools’ U15 Rugby Tournament, and to the Waikato. We feel very fortunate to have the role of host school this year.

We are delighted to welcome the 15 visiting schools in what is a unique tournament entering its 38th year. There is rich rugby history in all schools and the passion for the game is incredible. This tournament provides young men with a wonderful opportunity to compete against high calibre teams. Enjoy your time here, and the experience - remember why we are here and please let us know if we can help in anyway.

It is a pleasure this year for us to have this opportunity to host some of the very best school Rugby teams in New Zealand, and I wish everyone the very best in the days ahead. To be chosen for a junior premier Rugby team is an honour, and I congratulate every player participating, for winning a place in his school’s 2023 squad.

On behalf of the Principals and Headmasters of all of our schools, I thank you for your contribution, and I offer the thanks of all involved to your coaches and managers, for their commitment and passion, both for the very special game of Rugby, and for their students and schools.

My very best wishes to all; enjoy the competition, the opportunity to make new friends, to develop new skills, and to represent your school with pride.”

Mrs Susan Hassall, Headmaster
