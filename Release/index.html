Welcome

On behalf of the Waikato University, welcome to the 2023 U15 Secondary Schools Tournament. For those out of town, welcome to the beautiful Waikato - We hope you enjoy your time here and what our place has to offer.

We are delighted to welcome the 15 visiting schools in what is a unique tournament entering its 38th year. There is rich rugby history in all schools and the passion for the game is incredible. This tournament provides young men with a wonderful opportunity to compete against high calibre teams.

Enjoy your time here, and the experience - remember why we are here and please let us know if we can help in anyway.

HBHS Rugby & Sports Department.
